Kandu Valley Homestay Coorg

Tucked away in Kundauli Village, near the stunning Mallalli Falls in Coorg, Kandu Valley Homestay is more than just a place to stay — it’s a serene escape into nature. Surrounded by misty hilltops, quiet coffee estates, and the unmatched beauty of the Western Ghats, every corner of Kandu Valley tells a story of peace, simplicity, and connection with nature.

Whether you're visiting Coorg for a family trip, friends getaway, or office retreat, Kandu Valley Homestay provides the perfect space to relax, refresh, and reconnect with nature.
